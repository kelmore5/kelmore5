The First Blog Post

21 March 2016


Hey guys, welcome to my blog's very first (and albeit, probably anti-climatic) blog post! Woo!

As you've probably gathered from the home page, top bar, or footer, my name is Kyle Elmore, and I'm an aspiring freelance mobile developer. I've been researching how to get started as a freelancer over the past month, and this site/blog post is my start into learning and becoming a freelance mobile app developer.

So, over the next few weeks, I'm going to learn Android and iOS development concurrently, build up a few projects, and try to gain some work on Upwork.

I know it will more than likely be challening learning two different platforms at once, but I want to be versatile in my freelance work. Also, it seems that most of the projects on Upwork are looking for both Android and iOS applications, so I'm hoping to increase my chances of earning work there as well.

Also, while I've heard a lot of negative comments about Upwork and working for pennies (especially from the freelance section of reddit), I still think it's the best place to start freelance work. My hope is that after completing a few projects, I'll be able to gradually move off Upwork to either local projects or other work I can find.

Who knows? We'll see.

At any rate, I hope to be blogging more about mobile development, freelance work, and whatever other technological/programming related things I want to, so hopefully things go well. Thanks for reading!

Kyle Elmore, Freelance Mobile Developer
